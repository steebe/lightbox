# Lightbox

**A bright home for your negatives.**

Lightbox transforms your iPhone or iPad into a portable light table, giving film photographers a clean, bright backlight for reviewing and scanning negatives anywhere.

## Description

Lightbox displays a high-intensity white screen at adjustable brightness, purpose-built for placing film negatives against the display to inspect, photograph, or digitize them. An optional alignment grid helps keep negatives straight and evenly spaced. Controls collapse out of the way so nothing interrupts the light surface when you need full screen space.

## Features

- **Adjustable brightness** — Set backlight intensity from 30% to 100%
- **Alignment grid** — Toggle a grid overlay with spacing adjustable from 25pt to 100pt
- **Collapsible controls** — Hide the control panel for an unobstructed light surface
- **Minimal interface** — Distraction-free design that stays out of your way

## Compatibility

- Requires iOS 16.0 or later
- Compatible with iPhone and iPad
